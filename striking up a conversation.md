#dictionary #s 
**Definition:** "Striking up a conversation" refers to initiating or beginning a conversation with someone, often in a friendly or informal manner.

**Example:** Maria struck up a conversation with her new neighbor, asking about their favorite local restaurants.

**Synonyms:**
1. **Starting a dialogue**
2. **Initiating a chat**
3. **Engaging in a discussion**

**Antonyms:**
1. **Avoiding interaction**
2. **Ignoring**
3. **Remaining silent**
